# Mosman LGA Occupation Analysis - Professionals

## LGA Classification
Mosman is a **suburban** LGA located on Sydney's North Shore, known as one of Australia's most affluent residential areas with harbor frontage and proximity to Sydney CBD.

## Overall Assessment
Professionals are **significantly over-represented** in Mosman, which is typical for this affluent suburban area. The concentration of high-income professional occupations reflects the area's status as a premium residential location.

## Most Over-Represented Occupations

### 1. **Medical Specialists** (Multiple categories: 4-7x expected rates)
- Paediatric Surgeons, Neurosurgeons, Cardiologists, Dermatologists, Ophthalmologists
- **Indication**: Mosman attracts high-earning medical professionals who can afford premium housing, likely commuting to major Sydney hospitals

### 2. **Legal Professionals** (3-4.7x expected rates)
- Judges, Barristers, Solicitors, Tribunal Members
- **Indication**: Strong representation of senior legal professionals, reflecting both high incomes and proximity to Sydney's legal district

### 3. **Creative and Design Professionals** (2.4-3x expected rates)
- Architects, Interior Designers, Fashion Designers, Advertising Specialists
- **Indication**: Concentration of high-end creative professionals serving affluent clientele, with many likely running boutique practices

### 4. **Financial and Business Consultants** (2.4-3.1x expected rates)
- Management Consultants, Marketing Specialists, Company Secretaries
- **Indication**: Senior business professionals choosing prestigious residential location while maintaining Sydney CBD connections

## Most Under-Represented Occupations

### 1. **Registered Nurses (Aged Care)** (About one-third of expected)
- **Indication**: Limited aged care facilities in this premium residential area; aged care workers likely live in more affordable areas

### 2. **Registered Nurses (Mental Health)** (About half expected)
- **Indication**: Mental health facilities not concentrated in this affluent area; workers commute from other locations

### 3. **Environmental Health Officers** (About half expected)
- **Indication**: Government regulatory roles typically based elsewhere; workers live in more affordable areas relative to public sector salaries

## Summary
Mosman's professional occupation profile clearly reflects its status as Sydney's premier residential suburb for high-income earners. The concentration of medical specialists, senior legal professionals, and high-end creative consultants indicates a community of affluent professionals who can afford premium housing while maintaining professional practices in Sydney. This creates a distinctive enclave of Australia's highest-earning professional class.
